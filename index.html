Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how Andrii Lisnyk ("we", "us", or "our") handles your personal information when you use the Radix Log application.

1. Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

Radix Log is designed with privacy as a priority. All information you enter into the app (including plant names, photos, schedules, and journal entries) is stored locally on your device via Apple's Core Data / SwiftData technologies. We do not have servers, and we do not transmit your data to the cloud.

2. Photos and Camera Usage

The app requests access to your photo library and camera to allow you to add photos of your plants. These images are processed and stored locally on your device. They are never uploaded to any external server by us.

3. Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics services that track your behavior inside the app.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at chernnovaalbina489@gmail.com.
